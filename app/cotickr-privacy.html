Privacy Policy

Introduction

At CoTickr, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our team task management application.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (name, email address, password)
  • Profile information (profile picture, job title)
  • Task and project data
  • Communication data within the application

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To notify you about changes to our service
  • To provide customer support
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • To improve our application

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating our application
  • Law enforcement when required by law
  • Other team members within your organization (based on your settings)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Export your data

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date.
